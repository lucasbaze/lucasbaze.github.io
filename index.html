Human, Traveler, Creator of Things

"Sometimes I do. Mostly I don't."
- Lucas Bazemore

I really don't know what to put here. I have a ton of hobby ideas. Here are some of the most influential books that have made an impression on me. I like to make things. I started a startup that failed catastrophically. I started a student organization that failed. I've started a chess camp that failed. I've been a swim coach for my neighborhood. I REALLY wanted to be important, but I've recently realized that it's not worth it. I am at peace.
